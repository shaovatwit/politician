Frequently Asked Questions
Here are some frequently asked questions about this website.

This website is a data aggregator for local politicians. It is designed to make it easier for people to find information about their local politicians.

The data is currently scraped from the boston.gov website which is local to Sam and Vianne. In the future, it is hopefully going to be expanded! The data is scraped through using Python's library, BeautifulSoup with Django's Function-Based Views. All that information is then sent to our database, PostgreSQL. With the help of Django, we pull the information from the database and render it to the website you are currently accessing!

This website was created as a senior project for Sam Ellington and Vianne Shao. They got frustrated with the amount of work they had to put in in order to do research on candidates for city council every time an election rolled around. Now it is much easier to just go to this website and find all the information you need without having to navigate through pages after pages!

To use this website, simply navigate through the dropdown menu with information that pertains to you.

You can contact us through our emails: Vianne and Sam.
